Picnic Party

Weather Update: The forecast for Saturday is 16 degrees, intermittent light rain (3mm) and wind. Please wear warm clothes and good shoes, and perhaps pack an umbrella.

Summary: On the 17th of September we will hold a picnic party in the beautiful Mainzer Stadtpark to celebrate. Guests should arrive before 1pm and don't necessarily need to bring anything*. The main event will go until 5pm, and we might organise some evening festivities, if the demand is there.

*If you are bringing children, make sure to read all the details below.

Everything in the menu is completely vegan (yes, including the cheesecake). Individual dietary preferences can be fulfilled by bartering with other basket holders.

The baskets include everything: cutlery, plates, glasses, a blanket, napkins. There is no single-use items in the baskets.

Note for guests with children: We have not, by default, included any catering for children. Please bring food your children, or if you feel they are likely to eat "adult" food, then let us know so that we can include them.

The caterer will also provide water, juice and wines (red and white). Beer drinkers should bring their own, or quench their thirsts at the nearby Biergarten.

Guests are allowed to bring food to share. If you have something you want to bring, please let everyone know on the Padlet so we don't end up with multiples of similar dishes. The only exception is that nobody may operate a bbq grill at the picnic location.

Where?

The Mainzer Stadtpark (City park of Mainz) lies in the city of Mainz (itself which is on the river called the Main). Mainz is one of the cities neighbouring Frankfurt am Main.

By train: The Stadtpark can be reached very easily via the metro from Frankfurt. Take the S6 or S8 metro line from either the Hauptbahnhof or Hauptwache stations, in the direction of Wiesbaden. The ride takes about 40 minutes. Get off at the station Römisches Theater. The park is a short walk from there.

By car: The closest parking is at the south end of the Stadtpark and can be reached easily from the A60.

The Park Facilities

The Mainzer Stadtpark has a lot to offer us apart from a perfect picnic location and public bathrooms:

  • Aviary (free).
  • Flamingo exhibit (free).
  • Rose garden (free).
  • Water park, for kids and adults (free).
  • Rideable small train (1 euro per lap around the park).
  • Frisbee golf course (free).
  • Minigolf course (4 euro per game).
  • A Biergarten and a café (at your own cost).

Hey, um... What about Bad Weather?

In the case of light rain, we will still go ahead as planned. Between the market tents and trees there will be plenty of cover for everyone.

In the case of storm or excessive rain, we will of course cancel. However, such weather should come with several days of warning, so we will keep you informed if this is likely to happen.
