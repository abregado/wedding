The signing of the papers

You are invited to attend the very small and closed bureaucratic ceremony that is our official wedding. It will take place on the 14th September 2022. The time plan is as follows

  • 11:00am Meet at the Fountain of Justice
  • 11:30am Ceremony begins in the Town hall (Römer)
  • 12:00pm Take the tram to the Palmengarten
  • 1:30pm Lunch at the Cafe Siesmayer

Town hall (Römer)

Before the ceremony we will meet in the Frankfurt old city at the Fountain of Justice.

Town hall (Römer)

The "ceremony" is very minimalist and will take place in the Frankfurt Town hall (Römer). An official will say a few words in German, then we will sign some papers.

The current regulations allow us to bring the full amount of 17 guests into the signing. This may change if the Corona restrictions come back into force. Regardless, everyone will need to bring and wear a FFP-2 mask.

The whole ceremony will take about 20 minutes.

Palmengarten

After the ceremony we will, as a group, take the tram to the Palmengarten, which is a small botanical garden in Frankfurt. Here we can spend some time enjoying the park until lunch and thereafter.

Lunch

The Cafe Siesmayer is found inside (and technically outside) the Palmengarten.

Dress code

You can dress up and pretend this is a standard wedding if you like. Caro will have a dress and Ben will have a shirt and tie. Let's go with Comfortable Formal.
