Things to Bring
  • A soundbox and device to play music.
  • An easily sharable food item for the party. Be sure to list it on the Padlet and keep track of ingredients. It does not need to be vegan.
  • If you are coming by car, loan us a marquee or shelter tent for the day.
  • Swimming clothes for the water park.
  • Your championship putter for the Minigolf.
  • A Frisbee to play Frisbee golf with.
  • An outdoor game (like Kubb or Badminton) to play at the party.
  • Beer, if you want to drink but are not a wine person.
  • Bring a camera and take some pictures of the day for us.
